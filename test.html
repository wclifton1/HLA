Data Leaders

Big data and analytics are in inextricable part of the healthcare landscape, and H&I is no exception. There is more data than ever before, and organizing, mining, and presenting that data will be a challenge for every laboratory. HLA Data Systems brings a strong data team to help you solve your strategic data problems. We’ve committed to excellence in interoperability and data governance as pressures mount to include discrete data into the EMR. We’ve worked with centralized IT departments, the physician community, and EMR providers, so we know the challenges that can arise, and we’re able to deliver solutions that work for you.

HLA Advocates

We want to be your champion in the laboratory, in the clinic, and in the world of H&I. The regulatory environment is constantly evolving, as well as vendor requirements and hospital policies. It has become nearly impossible to keep up with the shifting demands of H&I. That’s where we come in. We know that choosing an LIS is a complicated and time-consuming process. And once the choice is made, it is difficult to transition. That’s why HLA Data Systems will partner with you in all things related to the HLA laboratory: interface projects, workflow improvement, user training, and much more.

Introducing mTilda
making lab life easier

mTilda is a Windows based LIS for the world of histocompatibility and immunogenetics. mTilda is used as the LIS of choice for some of the most innovative and process-intensive laboratories in the country. Through user-feedback and customer relationships, we continue to learn the needs of HLA labs. Countless expansions and improvements have been developed over the years, and Fred and Barbara have continued serving their clients thoughtfully and without fail.

Support and Services

Your HLA Advocates

When you have to call support, something has already gone wrong. mTilda support packages take a proactive approach, seeking out ways to make your lab life easier before something goes wrong.

New instruments, devices, and processes often require new procedures for capturing and reporting data, and oftentimes a lab doesn’t have the bandwidth to configure each new component, or they aren’t certain how their choices will affect efficiency in the lab.

mTilda is adaptable and configurable to your lab’s changing needs. We have a dedicated support team that is ready to answer your questions and solve your problems.
